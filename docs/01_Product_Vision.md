# 01 Product Vision — Quintessence

## Product purpose

Quintessence is a single-user Personal Operations System. It helps one person execute projects, build capabilities, create evidence, manage career opportunities, log decisions, and maintain a personal knowledge base.

## Problem statement

Personal execution is fragmented across documents, chats, notes, job applications, experiments, and task tools. This makes it difficult to know what is active, blocked, completed, useful as evidence, or worth turning into portfolio material.

## Success criteria

Quintessence succeeds when it becomes the first place checked when planning the day and the last place checked before ending it. It should make progress obvious, reduce forgotten commitments, and convert work into durable evidence.

## User personas

### Primary persona: REMÉ, personal operator

Needs a low-maintenance command center for projects, applications, capabilities, evidence, decisions, and learning.

### Secondary persona: Future REMÉ, reviewer and storyteller

Needs traceability from past work to evidence, resume bullets, capability proof, interview stories, and strategic decisions.

## Core workflows

1. Open Mission Control.
2. Review active projects, FGAs, opportunities, evidence, and decisions.
3. Update tasks and blockers.
4. Capture new evidence from work completed.
5. Link evidence to capabilities and opportunities.
6. Generate draft resume bullets and interview stories.
7. Record decisions and review them later.
8. Run weekly review.
